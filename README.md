# Cash_Forecasting
Building out solution to predict cash per month at a healthcare organization. 

This request was made by VP of Rev Cycle and the CFO to create a forecasting model to predict the amount of cash at the end of each month. Historically, the monthly projection was determined by aggrgating the total cash per year and divding by 12. 

The data is unique due to random large cash payments received from two distinct payors. These payments are so large that they appear to be outliers. This and the fact that zero payments occur on non-business days were two complications that were adjusted for to help the model succeed. 

The end result of the model's output performs fairly well; however, there is still work needed to tighten up the model and its predictions.
